NAVI Assistant

Your assistant for the development of apps using natural language
  • Get it on theCHROME WEB STORE
Integrated in AppInventor

NAVI Assistant automatically integrates into MIT App Inventor by simply installing the browser extension. NAVI monitors your every action to help you develop your apps

Easy to Use

Communicate with NAVI assistant using natural language to help you create your own application. All the assistant's responses are created to make you feel like you are talking to a real person

Adds new features

NAVI Assistant adds new features to enhance the user experience. For example, thanks to NAVI you can use step-by-step guided projects to learn how to create your own applications, or get an evaluation of your application.

NAVI Features

Evaluate your app to improve areas

You can get an evaluation of your app at any time. In the result of the evaluation you will get an overall grade of the project and you will know where you need to improve and how you can do it.

Troubleshoot problems while developing your app

If you have any problem during the development of the app, tell NAVI and they will help you to solve it.

Step by step guided project for learn how to use the platform

Develop with NAVI an app from scratch step by step, where you will first design your app and then give it logic thanks to the blocks.

Multi-language support

Support for communicating with NAVI in English and Spanish

Help you to improve a particular area of your app

NAVI can help you improve a particular area of your app. For example, writing: "How can I improve the user interface

Resolve questions while developing your app

NAVI provides information on the elements that are part of MIT App Inventor, and on how to perform certain basic actions
